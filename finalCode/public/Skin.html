Summary

Pinkish white skin

- Can wear all shades and genre of clothes.

- Should wear light-colored clothes.

- Choose a hot tone color.

- Better to wear Pastel colors, light blue, light pink and cream.

Tan skin

- Should wear neutral to cool tones or color which close to skin tone such as brown, khaki, gray, blue, purple or olive green.

- Should not put dark clothes, fresh colors and neon color.

White yellow skin

- Wear dark color clothes or light color clothes.

- Better to wear light orange, brown, light blue or dark red.

- Should not put dark grey, dark green and beige.

Honey skin

- Should wear cool tones, medium, light colors such as Turquoise, pinkish purple, beige, light brown, reddish brown and earth tone .

- Better to wear light green, carmine red or orange pink .

- Should not put warm colors and flashy colors .

TIPS

Should

- Use no more than 3 colors.

- Should match light shirt with dark pants or skirt. Otherwise, dark shirt with light pants or skirt.

- Match patterned clothes with medium tones or dark tones.

- Match cream with brown color.

- Use a neutral color such as gray .

- Match 1 full color with 1 neutral color .

- Match 1 full color with 2 neutral colors .

Shouldn’t

- Wearing too many pastel or soft colors .

- Wear clothes that colors look the same but are not the same such as brown and black .

- Use opposite colors .

- Wearing warm tones and cool tones in one set .

- Match orange shirt with blue pants

- Match green shirt with yellow pants

- Match black with dark blue
