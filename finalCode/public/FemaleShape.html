Choosing clothes for each body shape of woman

Rectangle

- Wear tops with details, such as a form-fitting tunic over the waist or a corset top.

- Clothes that should be worn such as tie-waist shirts, heart-shaped neck dresses, V-neck shirts, dresses with decorative pockets on the chest or shoulders.

- Should wear pants that are slightly flared, pants with a wider hip design than the rest, skinny Jeans, pants with back pockets.

- Should wear a skirt with a slightly flared end, pencil skirt in a high-waisted look, A-line skirt.

- Should wear a dress that has a scalloped shape to keep the shape around the waist,and the hem of the skirt is slightly flared.

- Shoes should be flat or high heels.

- The belt should be worn above the navel.

- Do not wear oversized or loose-fitting shirts, striped shirt, square neck shirt, shirt that is too tight.

- Shoes should not be worn with cut-out shoes.

Circle

- Should wear a Tunic shirt, knee-length coat or above the knee, wide neck/deep neck or jacket cropped.

- Should wear skinny jeans.

- Should wear a straight skirt, a slightly flared skirt, a slightly fitted skirt.

- The shoes that should be worn are solid high heels, ballet shoes, weave shoes or ankle boots.

- Do not wear a shirt with a vent at the waist, a shirt that is too tight, a shirt with a lot of details around the waist.

- Should not put the shirt in the pants or skirt.

- Should not wear pants or skirt that are flared, pleated and tapered.

- Avoid wearing spiked high heels, shoes that fit the shape of your feet.

Hourglass

- Should wear a shirt that is short enough to fit the waist, long pleated shirt, V-neck shirt, deep V-neck shirt, heart-shaped collar shirt, deep neckline, narrow neckline, trench coat or short jacket that floats above the hips.

- Shirts should be dark colored with long stripes or earth tones.

- Should wear plain pants, pants with pockets, pants that do not have closures or buttons that are too big, normal waist or high waist pants and flared pants.

- Should wear stretch or pleated skirts, A-line skirts, pencil skirts, round skirts or tulip skirts.

- Should wear a dress with a round skirt, dresses with a tulip skirt, dresses that are tight around the waist.

- Dresses should be earth tones.

- Should wear high heels.

- Accessories such as big bracelets are eye-catching, a long necklace that hangs down in a V shape, a long necklace with a pendant hanging near the chest or waist and a small belt.

- Should not wear a shirt with a vent or bow, boat neck and square neck shirt.

- Avoid pants that line the hips, low waist pants, skinny jeans(except slim legs) and solid skirts.

- Avoid flat shoes.

- Accessories that should not be worn such as necklaces that hang wide and low and big belts.

Pear

- Should wear a long coat, waist tie, boat neck, wide neck shirt, tunic style shirt, long blazer , shirts with top details(such as off-shoulder tops, ruffled tops), jacket.

- The colors that should be worn is black, navy blue, dark color, light color with dark bottom or earth tones.

- Should wear pants that fit, high waist pants or cylindrical pants.

- Suitable skirts are A-line skirts, fit and flare style skirts.

- Should wear a simple A-line dress, tie the waist or strapless dress.

- Should wear high heels.

- An accessory that fits in is a high-waisted belt.

- Should not wear shirts that emphasize the hips, flared pants, short skirts, puffy dresses , dress with many layers, dress with attached texture.

Inverted

- Should wear a V-neck shirt, oversize style shirt coat, single line shirt bell sleeve shirt, dress open one shoulder, short sleeve shirt design is quite fit or chubby figure.

- Should wear flared pants.

- When wearing a skirt or dress with an eye-catching pattern on the bottom, the top should be earth tones.

- When the upper part is dark, the lower part should be light.

- Should wear an A-line skirt, long skirt or pleated skirt.

- Should wear a V-neck dress or A-style dress.

- The shoes that should be worn is Ankle strap shoes.

- Should not wear open shoulders, round neck shirt, turtleneck, a shirt with ruffle details or a large shoulder detail, coat with details on the shoulder, short cropped jacket, crop top and a shirt with puff sleeves.

- Avoid small short legs, skinny fit pants, fitted skirts, jumpsuits with small barrels and tight-fitting straight dresses.

Apple

- Clothes that should be worn such as single-breasted, non-patterned jackets, fitted blazers, cardigans or vests with slits, over-the-knee coats, V-necks, slits, strapless, wide and deep necklines, high-waisted tops, smocked tops below the bust, A-line tops, tie-waist tops, hip-length tops, flowy tops that goes below the bottom, tunics, bell sleeves or cuffs, tops with cute patterns on the shoulders, such as sequins or flowers.

- Dresses that fit in such as A-line dress, patterned dress, most contrasting color dress or tie-waist dress.

- The shoes that should be worn are wedges, mid-length boots, ballet shoes, clock shoes, lace-up flats or high heels.

- Should not wear short tops, clothes with prominent patterns on the abdomen, tight tops, tops that show the back, tops with very high necklines, tops with decorative necklines, off-the-shoulder tops and boat-necked tops.

- Avoid low-waist pants, skinny jeans, leggings, pants that show a lot of zip in the front, skirts that are tight at the waist, dresses with smocks at the waist and tight dresses.

- The shoes that don't fit in such as flat shoes, belted boots and fluffy boots.

- An accessory that should be avoided such as clutch bags, shoulder bags and twine belts.
