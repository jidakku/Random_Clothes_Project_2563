Choosing an outfit for each occasion(Man)

Party

- Should choose a color theme that looks comfortable on the eyes earth tone.

- If you choose to wear a T-shirt, you should choose a smooth color cover up with a suit and pair it with slacks.

- Shorts suitable for casual weddings held by the sea or garden events.

- A pair of cool sneakers can be matched with an outfit to match the look.

- Wedding dresses focus on respecting the owner of the event, being polite and friendly.

- Do not wear vests, tuxedos and bow ties.

- Should not wear all black or all white.

Work

- Should choose to wear a long-sleeved shirt in earth tones or a comfortable color to pair with slacks.

- Shoes should be worn as cut-outs or polite sneakers.

- Do not wear casual clothing such as T-shirts, shorts or anything that looks too comfortable.

Go outside

- Dress as you like.

Travel

- Going to the mountains should dress completely. Maybe there's a hat or sunglasses, the shirt should be long-sleeved, the fabric should be durable. Also, the pants should be ankle-length. The fabric is durable. Shoes should be worn with closed-toe shoes, which could be worn to travel long distances.

- Going to the beach, the shirt can be a Hawaiian shirt or a comfortable T-shirt. Pants can be shorts or long pants but should be comfortable pants.

At home

- Dress as you like.

Exercise

- T-shirt or tank top stretch shorts are comfortable to wear, the fabric should be well ventilated.

- Do not wear clothes that are not stretchy and not suitable for exercise.
