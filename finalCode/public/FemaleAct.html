Choosing an outfit for each occasion(Woman)

Party

- Informal day party should wear a short dress or short skirt.

- Informal night party should be worn with a cocktail dress or a simple but elegant mini dress.

- Formal day party can be worn with short dresses or skirts, but they should be more polite.

- Semi-formal parties should wear knee-length dresses.

- Cocktail parties can be worn with mini dresses or skirts, but they should be about knee length.

- Do not wear all white or all black at the wedding.

Work

- Women's shirts and blouses for office wear should be simple without too much lace.

- Should choose long sleeves.

- Suitable dresses such as mini-dress, midi-dress, shirt dress or belted dress.

- Do not wear deep neckline T-shirts, tight-fitting T-shirts or sweaters.

Go outside

- Dress as you like.

Travel

- Should wear clothes that are worn and flexible such as Maxi dress and Cardigan.

At home

- Dress as you like.

Exercise

- Running outfits can be T-shirts and shorts or running pants, sports bras for running.

- Fitness-Weight Training might be a high impact sports bra, lightweight and moisture-wicking pants.

- Yoga and Pilates could be long pants that fit perfectly, tank tops, sleeveless tops, camisoles, shirts with built-in bras or long sports bras.
